Research Experience

Independent Researcher, National Taiwan University (2025-2026)
Conducted a year of independent research on TERRA, a telomeric repeat-containing long non-coding RNA. Showed how TERRA accumulates in UV-induced senescent cells, activates the MDA5/RIG-I innate immune pathway, and suppresses mitochondrial biogenesis via PGC-1α → NRF1/NRF2 → TFAM. First-author manuscript in preparation (expected 2027).
